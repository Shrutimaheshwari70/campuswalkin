📖 Overview
The Executive MBA programme at IIM Ahmedabad is crafted for seasoned professionals seeking to accelerate their journey into C-suite leadership. The curriculum integrates core management disciplines with advanced strategic frameworks, immersive case-based learning, and cross-border global modules. Participants emerge with sharper decision-making capabilities, a broader strategic lens, and lifelong access to one of Asia's most powerful alumni networks.
✅ Eligibility & Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree with a minimum of 50% marks or equivalent CGPA from a recognised university.
  • Minimum 5 years of full-time professional work experience post graduation.
  • Valid GMAT / CAT / GRE score as per current institute norms.
  • Compelling statement of purpose and two professional recommendations.
  • Personal interview round for shortlisted candidates.
