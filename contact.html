Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Just send us a message above.

Is the counselling service completely free?
Yes, 100%! All counselling sessions — whether via call, chat, or in-person — are completely free of charge. We never ask for payment for guidance.
How quickly will I get a response?
For WhatsApp and live chat, typically within minutes. For email and contact form submissions, our counsellors respond within 2 business hours during office hours.
Can I visit an office without an appointment?
Absolutely! You can walk in to any of our 4 offices during office hours. However, if you want a dedicated session with a specialist counsellor, booking a slot in advance is recommended.
Do you help with study abroad applications?
Yes! We have dedicated international admissions counsellors specialising in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, and Singapore. We help with university shortlisting, application essays, visa guidance, and more.
I have a complaint / feedback. Who do I contact?
We take feedback very seriously. Please email grievances@campuswalkin.com or use the contact form above selecting "Other" as the topic. Our customer success team will respond within 24 hours.
Can universities / institutions partner with CampusWalkin?
Yes! We work with 2,000+ institutions. For partnership enquiries, select "Partnership" in the contact form or email partnerships@campuswalkin.com. Our B2B team will reach out within 24 hours.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We are fully GDPR and IT Act compliant. Your data is never sold to third parties. It is used only to provide you with relevant guidance and program information. You can request deletion anytime.
Do you provide loan / financial assistance help?
Yes! We partner with leading education loan providers including Credila, Avanse, HDFC Credila, and government schemes. Our counsellors can guide you through the application process for free.
